Your data & privacy

What we store

  • Your email address (used for sign-in only)
  • Your gross salary and pay day setting
  • Your monthly pot names, amounts, and budget targets
  • Your saved monthly history — take-home, outgoings, mileage, and free money figures
  • Your app preferences — dark mode, email summary toggle, notification settings
  • Your inbox notifications and achievement badges

Where it's stored

All your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, in the europe-west2 region (London, UK). Nothing is stored on our servers — we don't have any. Your data goes directly between your device and Firebase.

What we never store

We never ask for, store, or have access to your bank login details, card numbers, account numbers, sort codes, or any banking credentials. This app does not connect to your bank.

Your rights

You can export all your data at any time using the export buttons on each saved month.

You can delete your account and all associated data permanently at any time. This cannot be undone.

Contact

Questions about your data? Email: hello@financetracker.app

Delete your account

This will permanently delete your account and all saved data including your full monthly history, pots, settings, and inbox. This cannot be undone.
