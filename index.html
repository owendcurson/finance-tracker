Accurate UK Tax Calculations

Income tax bands, personal allowance tapering, and employee NI — all calculated automatically using 2026/27 rates. See exactly what you take home.

Budget With Pots

Split your outgoings into named pots — rent, bills, food, savings — and see at a glance whether you're on track or over budget.

Form Your Accounts

Assign each pot to a bank account so you know exactly how much to transfer on payday. Direct payments, bills, savings — all sorted.

Mileage Claims

Log your business miles and get the tax-free amount calculated instantly at 55p per mile. Added on top of your take-home, not taxed.

Smart Payday Logic

Set your pay day and the app figures out weekends and UK bank holidays automatically. Shows your actual pay date and budget coverage window.
